Stray Kids Music Videos

MANIAC

"MANIAC" is the title track of Stray Kids 6th album, "ODDINARY". The song is about breaking through social norms and being unique by being a "maniac".

Video Published March 2022.

LALALALA

"LALALALA" is the title track of Stray Kids 8th album, "Rock-Star". The song talks about pushing through tough times in order to find joy, as most people do through listening to music.

Video Published November 2023.

Chk Chk Boom

"Chk Chk Boom" is the title track of Stray Kids 9th album, "ATE". The music video features Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman, as it came out around the same time as "Deadpool & Wolverine".

Video Published July 2024.
